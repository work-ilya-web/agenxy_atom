Naman is a New York based custom jewelry brand, offering over 100+ style variations, focused on the millenial and Gen Z demographic.

Founded by one of Agenxy’s investors, we were creating the brand from inception to final product.

The founder wanted Naman to be an interactive experience for the customer when shopping, so we decided to release a 3D display guide for each product, allowing consumers to have an even more in-depth look at Naman’s offerings. We managed their influencer campaigns, gathering and attracting more than 5 million followers to their brand in under 60 days, as well as managed their content curation shoots with models.
